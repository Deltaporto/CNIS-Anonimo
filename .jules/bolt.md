## 2025-02-14 - Replace Array.from with chunked fromCharCode.apply for large arrays
**Learning:** In `js/pdf-processor.js`, replacing `Array.from(bytes, byte => String.fromCharCode(byte)).join('')` and `.split('').map(char => char.charCodeAt(0))` with chunked `String.fromCharCode.apply(null, subarray)` and explicit `for` loop assignments (or `encodeLatin1`) yields massive performance gains (e.g. from 800ms+ down to ~70ms for 5MB arrays). The V8 engine struggles severely with large Array.from maps.
**Action:** When converting large typed arrays (like Uint8Array) to strings or vice versa in tight performance paths (such as PDF content stream processing), always avoid `Array.from` combined with functional maps. Prefer chunked `fromCharCode` or direct bounded loop assignment for typed arrays.
